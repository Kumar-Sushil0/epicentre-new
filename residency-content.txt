EPiCENTRE RESIDENCY PROGRAM
============================

THE SILENT IMMERSION
--------------------
7 days of guided solitude, deep work, and renewal.


OVERVIEW
--------
Return to Your Essence

The EPiCentre Residency is designed for those seeking a profound reset. It is not merely a vacation, but a structured period of withdrawal from the noise of modern life. Over the course of seven days, you will engage in a curriculum of silence, guided introspection, and communal being.

Our architecture, diet, and schedule are all calibrated to support your nervous system in down-regulating, allowing your natural clarity and creativity to surface effortlessly.

Program Highlights:
• 7 Days
• Small Group
• Guided
• Digital Detox


YOUR GUIDE
-----------
Neel Kothari
Lead Practitioner

With over two decades of experience in Vipassana and somatic healing, Neel brings a grounded, non-dogmatic approach to silence. His sessions focus on "active stillness"—teaching residents how to observe the mind without engaging with its chaos.

Specializations:
• Meditation
• Somatic Work
• Breath


TIMELINE
---------
Daily Rhythm

06:00 AM - Morning Bell & Nature Walk
Waking up with the sun. A silent, barefoot walk on the earth to ground the electrical body.

08:00 AM - Satvik Breakfast
Light, warm, digestion-friendly nourishment served in the communal hall.

10:00 AM - 01:00 PM - Deep Work / Contemplation
Unstructured time for journaling, reading, pottery, or simply sitting. No devices.

04:00 PM - Group Session with Neel
Guided meditation and somatic release techniques. Introduction to breathwork.

08:00 PM - Noble Silence & Rest
Retiring to quarters. Lights dimmed. Preparing the mind for dream states.
