# EPiCentre Resort - Admin Dashboard

Welcome to the **EPiCentre Resort Admin Dashboard**, a premium, high-end administrative interface designed for the management of our luxury resort and wellness retreat.

The admin dashboard provides a comprehensive suite of tools for resort administrators to manage every aspect of the guest experience, from bookings and room availability to wellness activities and events. Built with a focus on **visual excellence** and **user experience**, it adheres to the resort's premium brand identity using an earthy, gold-accented palette.

## ðŸš€ Key Modules

### 1. [Dashboard](/admin/dashboard)
- **Real-time Statistics**: Overview of revenue, bookings, user growth, and occupancy rates.
- **Recent Bookings**: Quick view of latest guest reservations.
- **Activity Log**: Chronological feed of administrative and user actions.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts for common tasks like adding rooms or creating events.

### 2. [Booking Management](/admin/bookings)
- **Detailed Tracking**: Full list of all bookings with status indicators.
- **Guest Profiles**: Access to guest contact information and special requests.
- **Financial Details**: Payment status and revenue breakdown per booking.

### 3. [User Management](/admin/users)
- **Role-based Access**: Manage admins, staff, and VIP members.
- **Guest Directory**: Comprehensive list of registered guests and their booking history.
- **Bulk Actions**: Export guest lists or manage multiple accounts simultaneously.

### 4. [Room Management](/admin/rooms)
- **Visual Grid/List Views**: Toggle between immersive room cards or data-dense tables.
- **Real-time Status**: Monitor room availability (Available, Occupied, Maintenance, Cleaning).
- **Amenity Management**: Detailed control over room features and pricing.

### 5. [Activities Management](/admin/activities)
- **Wellness & Adventure**: Manage resort experiences like Sunrise Yoga, Trekking, and Spa sessions.
- **Schedule Control**: Define available time slots and instructor assignments.
- **Capacity Tracking**: Monitor registration limits for each activity.

### 6. [Events Management](/admin/events)
- **Event Planning**: Organize workshops, festivals, and retreats.
- **Registration Tracking**: Visual progress bars for attendee capacity.
- **Venue & Organizer Management**: Assign locations and staff within the resort.

### 7. [Payment Management](/admin/payments)
- **Transaction History**: Detailed log of all financial interactions (Bookings, Activities, Events).
- **Payment Methods**: Support for Card, UPI, Net Banking, and Cash.
- **Status Monitoring**: Track successful, pending, failed, and refunded transactions.

### 8. [Blog Management](/admin/blogs)
- **Content Editor**: Full Markdown-supported editor for site blogs.
- **Engagement Metrics**: Track views, likes, and comments.
- **Publishing Workflow**: Manage Draft, Scheduled, and Published states.

### 9. [Analytics & Reports](/admin/analytics)
- **Revenue Trends**: Visual charts representing financial performance.
- **Demographics**: Insights into guest origins and preferences.
- **Performance Metrics**: Identify top-performing rooms and popular activities.

### 10. [Settings](/admin/settings)
- **General Config**: Site branding, contact info, and timezones.
- **Booking Rules**: Check-in/out times, cancellation policies, and deposits.
- **Security**: Password management and Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
